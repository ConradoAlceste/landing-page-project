Dengo chocolates are awesome

Dengo is a Brazilian manufacturer of specialty chocolates, produced with cocoa from Bahia by small and medium-sized producers, with the aim of reinvigorating cocoa cultivation in that region.

Cocoa beyond Chocolate Our motto: more cocoa, less sugar

Dengo was born to offer delicious, healthy and sustainable chocolates. Since day one, we have been dedicated to building a fairer chain.

Gianduia: a mix of intense creamy chocolate (60%) with coarsely grounded cashew nuts.

Macadamia, cashew nut and coconut dragees. Caramelized cocoa beans wrapped in 70% cocoa powder. Excellent to accompany a cup of coffee.

The pleasure of enjoying the pure chocolate flavor without other flavorings, produced only with cocoa butter, selected cocoa beans and less sugar. Intensity above 56% without milk.

Dengo: a word without translation, which crossed the Atlantic Ocean, from the African continent to Brazilian soil. In Portuguese, Dengo means kindness, affection, love and charm. Something special that certain people possess, that captivates and inspires others. It can also describe a gift you bring to surprise someone you love.
